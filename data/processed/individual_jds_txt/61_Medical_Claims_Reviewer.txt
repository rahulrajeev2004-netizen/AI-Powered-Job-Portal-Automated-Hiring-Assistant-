61. Medical Claims Reviewer 
Job Summary 
A Medical Claims Reviewer evaluates healthcare claims to ensure treatments are necessary, 
appropriate, and covered under insurance policies. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Review medical claims and patient records 
• Assess medical necessity of treatments 
• Approve or deny insurance claims 
• Identify fraud or misuse of healthcare services 
• Communicate with healthcare providers and insurers 
• Maintain compliance with insurance guidelines 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Experience in clinical or insurance sector 
Skills Required 
• Analytical thinking 
• Knowledge of medical procedures 
• Decision-making skills 
• Attention to detail 
Work Settings 
• Insurance companies 
• Third-party administrators (TPA) 
• Healthcare firms
