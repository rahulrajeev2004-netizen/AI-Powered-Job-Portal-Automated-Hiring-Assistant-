21. Cardiac Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Cardiac Nurse specializes in caring for patients with heart-related conditions such as 
coronary artery disease, heart failure, and post-cardiac surgery recovery. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor heart function (ECG, blood pressure, pulse) 
• Assist in cardiac procedures (angiography, angioplasty, bypass surgery) 
• Administer cardiac medications (anticoagulants, beta-blockers) 
• Educate patients on lifestyle changes (diet, exercise, smoking cessation) 
• Provide post-operative cardiac care 
• Recognize and respond to cardiac emergencies 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Cardiac nursing certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Strong knowledge of cardiology 
• ECG interpretation 
• Quick emergency response 
Work Settings 
• Cardiac care units (CCU) 
• Hospitals 
• Cardiology clinics
