37. Chief Nursing Officer (CNO) 
Job Summary 
The Chief Nursing Officer (CNO) is a top executive responsible for overall nursing services, 
strategy, and leadership within a healthcare organization. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Develop and implement nursing strategies and policies 
• Oversee all nursing departments and leaders 
• Ensure quality, safety, and compliance standards 
• Manage budgets and resource allocation 
• Represent nursing staff at executive level meetings 
• Drive innovation and improvement in patient care 
Qualifications 
• MSc Nursing / PhD (preferred) 
• Extensive leadership experience (10–15+ years) 
Skills Required 
• Strategic leadership 
• Financial and operational management 
• Policy development 
• High-level decision-making 
Work Settings 
• Large hospitals 
• Healthcare systems 
• Corporate healthcare organizations
