6. Patient Care Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Patient Care Nurse focuses on holistic care, ensuring physical, emotional, and 
psychological well-being of patients. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Develop and implement patient care plans 
• Monitor recovery progress 
• Coordinate with multidisciplinary teams 
• Educate patients on treatment and recovery 
• Ensure patient comfort and satisfaction 
• Handle patient queries and concerns 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
Skills Required 
• Patient-centered approach 
• Communication 
• Problem-solving 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Clinics 
• Home healthcare
