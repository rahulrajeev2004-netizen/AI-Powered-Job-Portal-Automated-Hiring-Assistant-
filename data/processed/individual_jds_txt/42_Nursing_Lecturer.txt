42. Nursing Lecturer 
Job Summary 
A Nursing Lecturer teaches nursing subjects at undergraduate and diploma levels, focusing 
on theoretical education and academic development. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Deliver lectures on nursing subjects 
• Prepare teaching materials and presentations 
• Conduct exams and assessments 
• Guide student research and assignments 
• Participate in academic planning and curriculum design 
• Stay updated with current medical advancements 
Qualifications 
• MSc Nursing (mandatory) 
• NET/PhD (preferred for higher institutions) 
Skills Required 
• Strong subject knowledge 
• Teaching and communication 
• Research aptitude 
Work Settings 
• Nursing colleges 
• Universities
