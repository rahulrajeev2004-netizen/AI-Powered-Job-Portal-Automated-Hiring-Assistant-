83. Health Inspector (Nursing Background) 
Job Summary 
A Health Inspector (with a nursing background) ensures public health safety and sanitation 
standards in communities. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Inspect sanitation, hygiene, and environmental conditions 
• Monitor food safety and water quality 
• Control spread of infectious diseases 
• Conduct health awareness programs 
• Enforce public health laws and regulations 
• Report health hazards and recommend corrective actions 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Diploma/Certification in Public Health or Sanitary Inspection 
Skills Required 
• Knowledge of public health laws 
• निरीक्षण (inspection) and analytical skills 
• Communication and reporting 
Work Settings 
• Municipal corporations 
• Public health departments 
• Government agencies
