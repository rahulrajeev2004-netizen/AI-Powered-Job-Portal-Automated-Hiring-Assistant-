39. Hospital Administrator (Nursing 
Background) 
Job Summary 
A Hospital Administrator with a nursing background manages overall hospital operations, 
combining clinical knowledge with administrative expertise. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Oversee hospital operations and departments 
• Manage budgets, staffing, and resources 
• Ensure compliance with healthcare laws and standards 
• Improve patient care services and hospital efficiency 
• Coordinate between medical, nursing, and administrative teams 
• Implement healthcare policies and programs 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing + MBA in Hospital/Healthcare Management (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Business and management skills 
• Leadership and decision-making 
• Financial planning 
• Communication and coordination 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Healthcare chains 
• Corporate healthcare organizations
