70. Dialysis Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Dialysis Nurse cares for patients with kidney failure, performing dialysis treatments and 
monitoring patient response. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Operate dialysis machines 
• Monitor vital signs during treatment 
• Educate patients about kidney health 
• Maintain dialysis equipment 
• Handle complications during procedures 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Training in dialysis procedures 
Skills Required 
• Technical skills 
• Attention to detail 
• Patient care and monitoring 
Work Settings 
• Dialysis centers 
• Hospitals
