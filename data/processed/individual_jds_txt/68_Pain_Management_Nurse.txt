68. Pain Management Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Pain Management Nurse specializes in assessing and treating acute and chronic pain 
using medications and therapeutic interventions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assess pain levels and causes 
• Administer pain relief medications and therapies 
• Monitor treatment effectiveness 
• Educate patients on pain control strategies 
• Assist in pain management procedures 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing 
• Certification in pain management (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Knowledge of analgesics and therapies 
• Patient assessment skills 
• Communication 
Work Settings 
• Pain clinics 
• Hospitals 
• Rehabilitation centers
