67. Palliative Care Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Palliative Care Nurse focuses on improving the quality of life for patients with serious or 
terminal illnesses by managing symptoms and providing emotional support. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Manage pain and other distressing symptoms 
• Provide end-of-life care and comfort 
• Support patients and families emotionally 
• Coordinate with multidisciplinary care teams 
• Educate families on disease progression and care 
• Assist in advance care planning 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Training in palliative or hospice care (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Compassion and empathy 
• Communication and counseling 
• Pain and symptom management 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Hospice centers 
• Home care services
