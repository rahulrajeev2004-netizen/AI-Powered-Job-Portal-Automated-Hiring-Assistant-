34. Mental Health Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Mental Health Nurse focuses on the emotional, psychological, and behavioral well-being 
of patients, supporting those with stress, anxiety, depression, and other mental health 
challenges. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide therapeutic communication and emotional support 
• Conduct mental health assessments 
• Assist in counseling and behavioral therapy programs 
• Monitor patient progress and recovery 
• Help patients develop coping strategies 
• Promote mental wellness and preventive care 
• Work with families and caregivers for holistic care 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Mental health training or certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Empathy and active listening 
• Counseling and interpersonal skills 
• Patience and adaptability 
• Problem-solving ability 
Work Settings 
• Community mental health centers 
• Hospitals 
• Schools and NGOs 
• Rehabilitation and wellness centers
