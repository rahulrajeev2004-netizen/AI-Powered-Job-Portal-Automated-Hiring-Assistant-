8. Clinical Nurse Specialist (CNS) 
Job Summary 
A Clinical Nurse Specialist is an expert in a specialized area of nursing practice (e.g., 
oncology, cardiology). They improve patient outcomes through clinical expertise, education, 
and system-level improvements. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide expert consultation on complex cases 
• Develop evidence-based care protocols 
• Train and mentor nursing staff 
• Improve quality of care and patient safety 
• Conduct clinical audits and research 
• Implement best practices in specialized units 
Qualifications 
• MSc Nursing (Specialization) 
• CNS certification (where applicable) 
Skills Required 
• Deep specialization knowledge 
• Teaching and mentoring 
• Analytical and research skills 
• Quality improvement expertise 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals (ICU, oncology, cardiology units) 
• Academic institutions 
• Research centers
