16. Rural Health Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Rural Health Nurse provides essential healthcare services in rural or underserved areas 
with limited access to medical facilities. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Deliver primary healthcare services 
• Conduct immunization and maternal health programs 
• Handle minor illnesses and injuries 
• Promote sanitation and hygiene awareness 
• Support government rural health initiatives 
• Refer serious cases to hospitals 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
Skills Required 
• Adaptability in low-resource settings 
• Problem-solving 
• Community engagement 
Work Settings 
• Rural clinics 
• Primary Health Centers (PHCs) 
• Mobile health units
