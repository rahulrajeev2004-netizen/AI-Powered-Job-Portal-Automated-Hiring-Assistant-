15. Home Health Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Home Health Nurse provides personalized medical care to patients in their homes, 
especially those with chronic illness, disability, or post-surgical needs. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor patient health and recovery 
• Administer medications and injections 
• Perform wound care and dressing 
• Assist with daily living activities 
• Educate patients and caregivers 
• Coordinate with doctors for home treatment plans 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
Skills Required 
• Independence and responsibility 
• Patient-centered care 
• Time management 
Work Settings 
• Patient homes 
• Home healthcare agencies
