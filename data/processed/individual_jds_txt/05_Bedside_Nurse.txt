5. Bedside Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Bedside Nurse provides direct, one-on-one patient care at the patient’s bedside, focusing 
on comfort, monitoring, and recovery support. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide continuous patient care 
• Monitor vital signs regularly 
• Assist with feeding, mobility, and hygiene 
• Administer medications 
• Provide emotional support to patients 
• Communicate patient updates to doctors 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
Skills Required 
• Compassion 
• Patience 
• Observation skills 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Home care settings
