66. NGO / Humanitarian Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Humanitarian Nurse works with NGOs to provide healthcare services in underserved, 
rural, or conflict-affected areas. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Deliver primary healthcare services 
• Conduct health awareness programs 
• Manage maternal and child health services 
• Support vaccination drives and disease prevention 
• Work in low-resource environments 
• Collaborate with global health organizations 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Experience in community/public health preferred 
Skills Required 
• Cultural sensitivity 
• Adaptability 
• Compassion and empathy 
• Resource management 
Work Settings 
• NGOs 
• International health organizations 
• Rural and remote areas
