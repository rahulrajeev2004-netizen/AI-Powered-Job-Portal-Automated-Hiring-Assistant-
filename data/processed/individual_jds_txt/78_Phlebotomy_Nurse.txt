78. Phlebotomy Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Phlebotomy Nurse specializes in drawing blood samples for diagnostic tests, transfusions, 
or donations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Collect blood samples safely and accurately 
• Label and handle specimens properly 
• Maintain hygiene and infection control 
• Prepare patients for blood collection 
• Monitor patients for adverse reactions 
• Maintain records and documentation 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM or Phlebotomy certification 
• Training in blood collection techniques 
Skills Required 
• Precision and steady hand 
• Knowledge of infection control 
• Communication skills 
• Attention to detail 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Diagnostic laboratories 
• Blood banks 
• Clinics
