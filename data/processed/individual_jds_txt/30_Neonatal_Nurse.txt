30. Neonatal Nurse (NICU Nurse) 
Job Summary 
A Neonatal Nurse cares for newborns, especially premature or critically ill infants, in 
Neonatal Intensive Care Units (NICU). 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor vital signs of newborns 
• Manage incubators, ventilators, and life-support equipment 
• Administer medications and IV fluids 
• Assist in neonatal procedures 
• Support feeding (breastfeeding, tube feeding) 
• Educate parents on newborn care 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• NICU/neonatal care training (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• High attention to detail 
• Gentle handling and care 
• Critical care skills 
Work Settings 
• NICU units 
• Maternity hospitals 
• Specialized pediatric hospitals
