49. Nursing Informatics Specialist 
Job Summary 
A Nursing Informatics Specialist integrates nursing science with information technology to 
improve patient care, data management, and healthcare systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Implement and manage Electronic Health Records (EHR) systems 
• Analyze clinical data to improve patient outcomes 
• Train healthcare staff on digital systems 
• Optimize workflow using technology solutions 
• Ensure data accuracy, privacy, and security 
• Collaborate with IT teams to develop healthcare software 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing + Certification in Nursing Informatics 
• MSc Nursing Informatics / Health Informatics (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• IT and data management knowledge 
• Analytical thinking 
• Problem-solving 
• Understanding of healthcare systems 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Health IT companies 
• Government health departments
