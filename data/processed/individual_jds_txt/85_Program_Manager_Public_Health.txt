85. Program Manager (Public Health 
Nursing) 
Job Summary 
A Program Manager oversees public health programs, ensuring effective planning, 
implementation, and evaluation. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Plan and manage healthcare programs (e.g., vaccination, maternal health) 
• Coordinate with healthcare teams and stakeholders 
• Monitor program performance and outcomes 
• Manage budgets and resources 
• Ensure compliance with government policies 
• Prepare reports and evaluations 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing 
• MPH / Healthcare Management (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Leadership and management 
• Organizational skills 
• Data analysis 
• Communication 
Work Settings 
• Government health departments 
• NGOs 
• International health agencies
