2. Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN) / 
Vocational Nurse 
Job Summary 
LPNs provide basic nursing care under the supervision of RNs and doctors, focusing on 
routine patient care and comfort. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor vital signs (BP, temperature, pulse) 
• Assist patients with daily activities (bathing, feeding) 
• Administer basic medications (as permitted) 
• Dress wounds and assist in procedures 
• Maintain hygiene and patient comfort 
• Report patient status to RNs 
Qualifications 
• Diploma in Practical Nursing / GNM equivalent 
Skills Required 
• Basic clinical skills 
• Attention to detail 
• Patient care and support 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Long-term care facilities 
• Rehabilitation centers
