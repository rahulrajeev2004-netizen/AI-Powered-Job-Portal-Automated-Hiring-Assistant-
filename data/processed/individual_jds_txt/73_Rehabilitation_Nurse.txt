73. Rehabilitation Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Rehabilitation Nurse helps patients recover from injuries, surgeries, or disabilities, 
focusing on restoring function and independence. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Develop rehabilitation care plans 
• Assist with physical therapy programs 
• Monitor patient progress 
• Educate patients on recovery techniques 
• Support long-term care needs 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing 
• Training in rehabilitation care (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Patience and motivation skills 
• Physical rehabilitation knowledge 
• Communication 
Work Settings 
• Rehabilitation centers 
• Hospitals 
• Long-term care facilities
