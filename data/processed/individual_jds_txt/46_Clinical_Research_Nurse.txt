46. Clinical Research Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Clinical Research Nurse manages and participates in clinical trials, ensuring patient safety 
and compliance with research protocols. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Recruit and screen participants for clinical trials 
• Obtain informed consent 
• Administer study treatments and interventions 
• Monitor patient responses and side effects 
• Maintain accurate clinical trial documentation 
• Ensure compliance with ethical and regulatory standards 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Training in clinical research (GCP certification preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Knowledge of clinical trials 
• Attention to detail 
• Ethical and regulatory understanding 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Pharmaceutical companies 
• Clinical research organizations (CROs)
