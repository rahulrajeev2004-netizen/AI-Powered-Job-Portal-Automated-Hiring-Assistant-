20. Critical Care Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Critical Care Nurse provides specialized care for patients with life-threatening conditions 
across ICUs, emergency units, and critical care departments. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Continuously monitor critically ill patients 
• Administer complex medication regimens 
• Manage advanced medical equipment 
• Support organ function (cardiac, respiratory, renal) 
• Assist in invasive procedures 
• Provide patient and family education 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Critical care specialization (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Advanced clinical expertise 
• Analytical thinking 
• Emotional resilience 
• Team coordination 
Work Settings 
• ICUs (Cardiac, Neuro, Surgical) 
• Critical care units 
• Emergency departments
