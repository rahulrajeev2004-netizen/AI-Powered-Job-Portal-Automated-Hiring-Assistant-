47. Research Coordinator (Nursing) 
Job Summary 
A Research Coordinator manages the operational aspects of research projects and clinical 
studies, ensuring smooth execution from start to completion. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Coordinate research project activities 
• Manage documentation and regulatory submissions 
• Schedule patient visits and follow-ups 
• Communicate with sponsors and research teams 
• Ensure compliance with study protocols 
• Track project timelines and progress 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing 
• Clinical research certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Organizational and project management skills 
• Communication and coordination 
• Documentation and compliance knowledge 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• CROs 
• Academic research centers
