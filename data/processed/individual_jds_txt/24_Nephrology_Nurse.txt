24. Nephrology Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Nephrology Nurse specializes in caring for patients with kidney diseases and those 
undergoing dialysis. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform and monitor dialysis (hemodialysis/peritoneal) 
• Monitor fluid balance and electrolyte levels 
• Administer medications related to kidney function 
• Educate patients on diet and lifestyle changes 
• Manage vascular access (fistulas, catheters) 
• Monitor for complications during dialysis 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Dialysis/nephrology training (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Technical dialysis skills 
• Precision and monitoring ability 
• Patient education 
Work Settings 
• Dialysis centers 
• Hospitals 
• Nephrology departments
