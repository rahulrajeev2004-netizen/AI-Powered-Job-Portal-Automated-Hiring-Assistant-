35. Nurse Manager 
Job Summary 
A Nurse Manager oversees a specific unit or department in a hospital, ensuring high-quality 
patient care while managing nursing staff and daily operations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Supervise and coordinate nursing staff 
• Manage duty schedules and staffing levels 
• Ensure quality patient care and safety standards 
• Handle patient complaints and resolve issues 
• Monitor performance and conduct staff evaluations 
• Maintain inventory of medical supplies 
• Implement hospital policies and protocols 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing (MSc Nursing preferred) 
• Clinical experience (3–5+ years) 
Skills Required 
• Leadership and team management 
• Problem-solving 
• Communication and conflict resolution 
• Organizational skills 
Work Settings 
• Hospital departments (ICU, wards, OPD) 
• Clinics 
• Nursing homes
