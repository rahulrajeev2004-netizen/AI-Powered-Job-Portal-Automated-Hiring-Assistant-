74. Geriatric Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Geriatric Nurse specializes in caring for elderly patients, managing age-related health 
conditions and promoting quality of life. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assess and manage chronic conditions 
• Assist with daily living activities 
• Monitor medications and treatments 
• Provide emotional support 
• Educate families on elderly care 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Specialization in geriatric care (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Patience and empathy 
• Knowledge of age-related diseases 
• Communication skills 
Work Settings 
• Nursing homes 
• Hospitals 
• Home care services
