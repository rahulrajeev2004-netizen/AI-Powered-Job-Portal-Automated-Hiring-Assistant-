22. Oncology Nurse 
Job Summary 
An Oncology Nurse provides care to patients diagnosed with cancer, supporting them 
through diagnosis, treatment, and recovery or palliative care. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Administer chemotherapy, immunotherapy, and targeted treatments 
• Monitor and manage treatment side effects 
• Provide emotional and psychological support 
• Educate patients and families about cancer care 
• Coordinate with oncologists and multidisciplinary teams 
• Assist in palliative and end-of-life care 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Oncology nursing certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Compassion and emotional resilience 
• Knowledge of cancer treatments 
• Patient counseling 
Work Settings 
• Cancer hospitals 
• Oncology departments 
• Palliative care centers
