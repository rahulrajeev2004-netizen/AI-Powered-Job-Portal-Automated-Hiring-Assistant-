12. Public Health Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Public Health Nurse works at a broader population level, developing and implementing 
health policies and programs to improve overall public health. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and implement public health programs 
• Monitor disease trends and epidemiological data 
• Conduct health awareness campaigns 
• Work on national programs (TB, HIV, maternal health) 
• Collaborate with government agencies 
• Evaluate health program effectiveness 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing + MPH (Master of Public Health preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Analytical and research skills 
• Policy understanding 
• Leadership and planning 
Work Settings 
• Government health departments 
• International organizations (WHO, NGOs) 
• Research institutions
