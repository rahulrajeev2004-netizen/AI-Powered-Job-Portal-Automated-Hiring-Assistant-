72. Cosmetic / Aesthetic Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Cosmetic Nurse provides beauty and aesthetic treatments such as Botox, fillers, and skin 
therapies. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform cosmetic procedures (injections, skincare treatments) 
• Assess patient suitability for procedures 
• Educate clients on skincare and aftercare 
• Maintain hygiene and safety standards 
• Assist dermatologists or cosmetic surgeons 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing 
• Certification in aesthetic procedures 
Skills Required 
• Precision and attention to detail 
• Aesthetic sense 
• Communication and client handling 
Work Settings 
• Cosmetic clinics 
• Dermatology centers 
• Medical spas
