76. Patient Care Technician (PCT) 
Job Summary 
A Patient Care Technician provides advanced support to nurses, performing both basic care 
and some clinical tasks. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assist with patient care (hygiene, mobility, feeding) 
• Measure and record vital signs 
• Perform basic clinical tasks (ECG, specimen collection) 
• Assist in emergency situations 
• Prepare patients for procedures 
• Maintain medical equipment and supplies 
Qualifications 
• PCT certification or diploma 
• Basic nursing or paramedical training 
Skills Required 
• Clinical and technical skills 
• Attention to detail 
• Ability to work under pressure 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Emergency departments 
• Dialysis centers
