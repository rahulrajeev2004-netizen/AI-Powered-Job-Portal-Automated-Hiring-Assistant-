3. Nursing Officer 
Job Summary 
A Nursing Officer is a government or institutional role responsible for delivering patient care 
while also handling administrative and supervisory tasks. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide clinical nursing care 
• Supervise junior nurses and staff 
• Ensure adherence to hospital protocols 
• Maintain medical records and reports 
• Assist in hospital administration 
• Participate in policy implementation 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing (preferred) 
• Registered Nurse certification 
• Often requires government exam qualification 
Skills Required 
• Leadership 
• Clinical expertise 
• Organizational skills 
Work Settings 
• Government hospitals 
• Defense services 
• Public health institutions
