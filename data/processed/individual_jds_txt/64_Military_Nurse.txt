64. Military Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Military Nurse serves in the armed forces, providing care to soldiers, veterans, and 
sometimes civilians in military or field settings. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Deliver medical care in hospitals or field units 
• Assist in surgeries and trauma care 
• Support battlefield medical operations 
• Provide rehabilitation and long-term care 
• Participate in military training and missions 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing 
• Must meet military recruitment criteria 
• Physical fitness standards required 
Skills Required 
• Discipline and leadership 
• Emergency and trauma care skills 
• Physical and mental resilience 
• Teamwork 
Work Settings 
• Military hospitals 
• Field hospitals 
• War zones or peacekeeping missions
