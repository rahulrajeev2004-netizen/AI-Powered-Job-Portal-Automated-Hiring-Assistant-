51. Clinical Data Analyst (Nursing 
Background) 
Job Summary 
A Clinical Data Analyst with a nursing background analyzes healthcare data to improve 
patient outcomes, hospital performance, and clinical decision-making. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Collect and analyze clinical data 
• Identify trends in patient outcomes and treatments 
• Prepare reports and dashboards 
• Support evidence-based decision-making 
• Ensure data quality and compliance 
• Collaborate with healthcare and IT teams 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing + Data Analytics certification 
• Knowledge of tools (Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI – preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Data analysis and statistics 
• Critical thinking 
• Technical skills 
• Attention to detail 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Healthcare analytics companies 
• Insurance companies
