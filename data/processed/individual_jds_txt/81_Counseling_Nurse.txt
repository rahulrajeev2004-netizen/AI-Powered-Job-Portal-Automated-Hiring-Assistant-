81. Counseling Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Counseling Nurse provides guidance, emotional support, and therapeutic 
communication to patients dealing with stress, illness, trauma, or life challenges. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Conduct counseling sessions for patients and families 
• Assess emotional and psychological needs 
• Help patients cope with illness, stress, or trauma 
• Provide mental health education and awareness 
• Support lifestyle and behavioral changes 
• Refer patients to specialists when needed 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Certification in counseling or psychology (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Active listening and empathy 
• Counseling and interpersonal skills 
• Problem-solving 
• Emotional intelligence 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Clinics 
• Schools and colleges 
• NGOs and wellness centers
