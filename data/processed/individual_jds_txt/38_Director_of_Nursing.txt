38. Director of Nursing 
Job Summary 
The Director of Nursing is responsible for managing the entire nursing department, focusing 
on operational efficiency, staff development, and quality care delivery. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Oversee nursing operations across the facility 
• Develop staffing plans and training programs 
• Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations 
• Monitor patient care quality and safety 
• Manage budgets and departmental resources 
• Lead quality improvement initiatives 
Qualifications 
• MSc Nursing (mandatory in most cases) 
• Leadership experience 
Skills Required 
• Leadership and administration 
• Strategic planning 
• Quality management 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Healthcare institutions 
• Long-term care facilities
