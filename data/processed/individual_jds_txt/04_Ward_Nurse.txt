4. Ward Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Ward Nurse manages patient care within a specific hospital ward, ensuring smooth day-to
day operations and patient monitoring. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor all patients in the assigned ward 
• Administer medications and treatments 
• Coordinate with doctors for patient care 
• Maintain ward cleanliness and discipline 
• Supervise ward assistants and junior staff 
• Handle patient admissions and discharges 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
Skills Required 
• Multitasking 
• Coordination 
• Time management 
Work Settings 
• Hospital wards (medical, surgical, pediatric, etc.)
