13. School Nurse 
Job Summary 
A School Nurse ensures the health and well-being of students and staff within educational 
institutions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide first aid and emergency care 
• Manage chronic conditions (asthma, diabetes) 
• Conduct health screenings and check-ups 
• Maintain student health records 
• Educate students on hygiene and nutrition 
• Handle outbreaks (flu, infections) 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
Skills Required 
• Child care skills 
• Communication and patience 
• Emergency response 
Work Settings 
• Schools 
• Colleges 
• Educational institutions
