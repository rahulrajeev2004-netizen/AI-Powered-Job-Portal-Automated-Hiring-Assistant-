11. Community Health Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Community Health Nurse provides healthcare services to individuals, families, and groups 
within a community, focusing on disease prevention, health promotion, and basic treatment. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Conduct community health assessments and surveys 
• Provide basic medical care and first aid 
• Organize vaccination and immunization programs 
• Educate the public on hygiene, nutrition, and disease prevention 
• Monitor outbreaks and report public health issues 
• Coordinate with local health authorities 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Community health training (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Communication and teaching skills 
• Cultural awareness 
• Preventive healthcare knowledge 
Work Settings 
• Community centers 
• Government health programs 
• NGOs
