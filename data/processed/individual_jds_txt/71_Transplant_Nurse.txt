71. Transplant Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Transplant Nurse manages care for patients undergoing organ transplants, from pre
surgery preparation to post-operative recovery. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Prepare patients for transplant surgery 
• Monitor post-transplant recovery 
• Educate patients on medication and lifestyle changes 
• Coordinate with transplant teams 
• Monitor for organ rejection 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing 
• Experience in critical care or transplant units 
Skills Required 
• Critical care knowledge 
• Coordination skills 
• Patient education 
Work Settings 
• Specialized transplant centers 
• Hospitals
