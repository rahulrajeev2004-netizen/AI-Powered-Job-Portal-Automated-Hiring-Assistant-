29. Pediatric Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Pediatric Nurse provides medical care to infants, children, and adolescents, focusing on 
growth, development, and treatment of childhood illnesses. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor physical and developmental milestones 
• Administer vaccinations and medications 
• Treat common childhood illnesses and injuries 
• Assist in pediatric procedures and diagnostics 
• Educate parents on child health, nutrition, and hygiene 
• Provide emotional support to children and families 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Pediatric nursing training (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Child-friendly communication 
• Patience and empathy 
• Observation and assessment skills 
Work Settings 
• Pediatric wards 
• Children’s hospitals 
• Clinics
