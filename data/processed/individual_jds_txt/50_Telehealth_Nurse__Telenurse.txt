50. Telehealth Nurse / Telenurse 
Job Summary 
A Telehealth Nurse provides remote healthcare services through phone, video calls, or digital 
platforms, improving access to care. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Conduct virtual patient consultations 
• Assess symptoms and provide medical advice 
• Monitor chronic conditions remotely 
• Educate patients on medications and lifestyle 
• Coordinate care with doctors and specialists 
• Maintain digital health records 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Telehealth training (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Communication and listening skills 
• Digital literacy 
• Clinical assessment skills 
Work Settings 
• Telemedicine platforms 
• Hospitals 
• Home healthcare services
