75. Nursing Assistant (CNA – Certified 
Nursing Assistant) 
Job Summary 
A Nursing Assistant provides basic patient care and support under the supervision of 
registered nurses and doctors. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assist patients with daily activities (bathing, dressing, feeding) 
• Monitor vital signs (temperature, pulse, blood pressure) 
• Help patients move, turn, or walk 
• Maintain cleanliness and hygiene of patients 
• Assist nurses with routine procedures 
• Provide emotional support and companionship 
Qualifications 
• Nursing assistant certification (CNA course) 
• Basic healthcare training 
Skills Required 
• Compassion and patience 
• Basic clinical skills 
• Communication skills 
• Physical stamina 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Nursing homes 
• Rehabilitation centers 
• Home care services
