69. Infection Control Nurse 
Job Summary 
An Infection Control Nurse works to prevent and control infections within healthcare 
settings, ensuring patient and staff safety. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor infection rates and outbreaks 
• Develop infection prevention protocols 
• Train staff on hygiene and safety practices 
• Conduct audits and inspections 
• Ensure compliance with health regulations 
• Manage hospital infection control programs 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing 
• Certification in infection control (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Knowledge of microbiology and infection control 
• Analytical skills 
• Attention to detail 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Public health organizations 
• Laboratories
