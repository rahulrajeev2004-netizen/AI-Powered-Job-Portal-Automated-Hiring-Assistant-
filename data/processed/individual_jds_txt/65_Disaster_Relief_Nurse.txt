65. Disaster Relief Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Disaster Relief Nurse provides emergency healthcare during natural disasters, 
pandemics, or crises. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Deliver urgent care in disaster zones 
• Triage patients based on severity 
• Manage injuries, infections, and trauma 
• Coordinate with emergency response teams 
• Provide vaccinations and disease control 
• Support affected communities 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Training in disaster management/emergency care 
Skills Required 
• Crisis management 
• Quick decision-making 
• Emotional resilience 
• Team coordination 
Work Settings 
• Disaster zones 
• Relief camps 
• Emergency response organizations
