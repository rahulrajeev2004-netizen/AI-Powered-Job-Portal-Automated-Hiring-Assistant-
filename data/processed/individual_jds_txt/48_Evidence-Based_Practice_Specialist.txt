48. Evidence-Based Practice Specialist 
Job Summary 
An Evidence-Based Practice (EBP) Specialist ensures that patient care and nursing practices 
are based on the latest scientific evidence and best practices. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Review and analyze current research studies 
• Develop and implement evidence-based guidelines 
• Train staff on updated clinical practices 
• Evaluate patient care outcomes 
• Improve quality and safety standards 
• Bridge gap between research and clinical practice 
Qualifications 
• MSc Nursing (preferred) 
• Training in research and quality improvement 
Skills Required 
• Analytical and critical thinking 
• Research interpretation 
• Quality improvement knowledge 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Academic institutions 
• Healthcare quality departments
