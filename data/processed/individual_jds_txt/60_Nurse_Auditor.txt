60. Nurse Auditor 
Job Summary 
A Nurse Auditor reviews healthcare records and processes to ensure compliance with medical 
standards, billing accuracy, and regulatory requirements. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Audit patient records and treatment documentation 
• Ensure compliance with healthcare laws and policies 
• Verify billing accuracy and coding 
• Identify discrepancies or fraud 
• Prepare audit reports and recommendations 
• Improve quality and compliance systems 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Certification in medical auditing/coding (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Attention to detail 
• Knowledge of healthcare regulations 
• Analytical and auditing skills 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Insurance companies 
• Government agencies
