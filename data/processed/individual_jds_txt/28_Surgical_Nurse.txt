28. Surgical Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Surgical Nurse focuses on caring for patients undergoing surgical treatment, primarily in 
pre-operative and post-operative phases. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Prepare patients for surgery (tests, consent, education) 
• Monitor vital signs and patient condition post-surgery 
• Perform wound care and dressing 
• Administer medications and pain management 
• Detect and manage post-surgical complications (infection, bleeding) 
• Educate patients on recovery and rehabilitation 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
Skills Required 
• Patient monitoring 
• Wound care expertise 
• Post-operative care knowledge 
• Attention to detail 
Work Settings 
• Surgical wards 
• Hospitals 
• Recovery units
