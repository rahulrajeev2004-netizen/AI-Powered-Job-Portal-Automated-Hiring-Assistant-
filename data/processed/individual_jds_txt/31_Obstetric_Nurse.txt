31. Obstetric Nurse 
Job Summary 
An Obstetric Nurse provides care to women during pregnancy (prenatal phase), ensuring the 
health of both mother and fetus. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Conduct prenatal assessments and checkups 
• Monitor fetal development and maternal health 
• Assist in diagnostic tests (ultrasound support, lab tests) 
• Educate mothers on pregnancy care and nutrition 
• Identify high-risk pregnancies and complications 
• Coordinate with obstetricians 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Obstetrics & gynecology training (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Maternal care expertise 
• Communication and counseling 
• Risk assessment 
Work Settings 
• Maternity clinics 
• Hospitals 
• Community health centers
