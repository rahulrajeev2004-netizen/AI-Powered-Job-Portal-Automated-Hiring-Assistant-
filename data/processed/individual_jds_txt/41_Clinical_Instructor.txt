41. Clinical Instructor 
Job Summary 
A Clinical Instructor supervises nursing students during their practical training in hospitals, 
ensuring safe and effective patient care practices. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Guide students during clinical rotations 
• Demonstrate nursing procedures 
• Monitor student performance in real patient settings 
• Provide feedback and evaluations 
• Ensure adherence to safety and ethical standards 
• Bridge theory and practical application 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing 
• Clinical experience (required) 
Skills Required 
• Hands-on clinical expertise 
• Mentoring ability 
• Observation and evaluation skills 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Teaching hospitals 
• Nursing colleges
