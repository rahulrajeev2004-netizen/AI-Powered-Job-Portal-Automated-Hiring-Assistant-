52. Healthcare AI Trainer (Nursing) 
Job Summary 
A Healthcare AI Trainer uses clinical knowledge to train, validate, and improve artificial 
intelligence models used in healthcare systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Annotate and label medical data for AI training 
• Validate AI outputs for accuracy and safety 
• Collaborate with AI engineers and developers 
• Train AI systems on clinical workflows and decision-making 
• Ensure ethical use of healthcare AI 
• Improve AI performance using real-world clinical insights 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing 
• Basic knowledge of AI/ML concepts (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Clinical expertise 
• Analytical thinking 
• Attention to detail 
• Basic tech understanding 
Work Settings 
• Health-tech companies 
• AI startups 
• Research labs
