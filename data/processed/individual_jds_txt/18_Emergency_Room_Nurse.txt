18. Emergency Room (ER) Nurse 
Job Summary 
An ER Nurse provides immediate care to patients with acute illnesses or injuries in 
emergency departments, often dealing with life-threatening situations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform triage to prioritize patient care 
• Provide emergency treatment (CPR, wound care, stabilization) 
• Assist doctors in emergency procedures 
• Administer medications quickly and accurately 
• Monitor patient condition continuously 
• Prepare patients for surgery or admission 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Emergency care training (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Fast decision-making 
• Multitasking 
• Crisis and stress management 
• Strong communication 
Work Settings 
• Emergency departments 
• Trauma centers 
• Ambulance services
