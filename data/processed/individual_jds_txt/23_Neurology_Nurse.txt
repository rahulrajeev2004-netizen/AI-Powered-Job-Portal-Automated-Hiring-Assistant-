23. Neurology Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Neurology Nurse cares for patients with disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous 
system such as stroke, epilepsy, and Parkinson’s disease. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor neurological status (consciousness, reflexes, motor function) 
• Assist in stroke management and rehabilitation 
• Administer medications for neurological disorders 
• Support patients with mobility and coordination issues 
• Educate families on long-term neurological care 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Neurology specialization (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Neurological assessment skills 
• Observation and attention to detail 
• Patience and empathy 
Work Settings 
• Neurology wards 
• Stroke units 
• Rehabilitation centers
