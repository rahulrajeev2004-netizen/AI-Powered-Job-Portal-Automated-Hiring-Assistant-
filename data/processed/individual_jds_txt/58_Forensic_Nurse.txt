58. Forensic Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Forensic Nurse provides medical care to victims of crime while also collecting and 
preserving evidence for legal investigations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Examine victims of assault, abuse, or violence 
• Collect forensic evidence (DNA samples, injury documentation) 
• Maintain chain of custody for legal evidence 
• Provide medical treatment and emotional support 
• Document findings for legal use 
• Testify in court as an expert witness (in some cases) 
• Collaborate with law enforcement and legal authorities 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Forensic nursing certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Attention to detail 
• Knowledge of legal procedures 
• Emotional resilience 
• Documentation accuracy 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals (emergency/forensic units) 
• Forensic labs 
• Law enforcement agencies
