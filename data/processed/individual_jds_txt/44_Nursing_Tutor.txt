44. Nursing Tutor 
Job Summary 
A Nursing Tutor provides foundational education and guidance to nursing students, 
especially at diploma and entry-level programs. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Teach basic nursing subjects and skills 
• Assist students with practical learning 
• Conduct demonstrations and tutorials 
• Evaluate student progress 
• Support students in skill development 
• Assist senior faculty 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing (MSc preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Basic teaching skills 
• Patience and mentoring 
• Clinical knowledge 
Work Settings 
• Nursing schools 
• Training institutes
