79. Addiction Nurse 
Job Summary 
An Addiction Nurse specializes in caring for patients struggling with substance abuse and 
addiction (alcohol, drugs, prescription misuse), supporting detoxification, recovery, and 
relapse prevention. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assess patients for substance use disorders 
• Monitor patients during detox and withdrawal 
• Administer medications for addiction treatment (e.g., withdrawal management) 
• Provide counseling and emotional support 
• Educate patients and families about addiction and recovery 
• Develop relapse prevention strategies 
• Collaborate with psychiatrists, therapists, and rehab teams 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Training or certification in addiction/psychiatric nursing (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Strong counseling and communication skills 
• Patience and empathy 
• Crisis management 
• Knowledge of addiction medicine 
Work Settings 
• De-addiction centers 
• Rehabilitation facilities 
• Hospitals 
• Mental health clinics
