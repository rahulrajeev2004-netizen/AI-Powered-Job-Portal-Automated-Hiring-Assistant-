10. Nurse Midwife (CNM) 
Job Summary 
A Certified Nurse Midwife (CNM) specializes in women’s reproductive health, pregnancy, 
childbirth, and postpartum care, often providing holistic and patient-centered maternity 
services. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide prenatal care and health assessments 
• Monitor fetal development 
• Assist and manage normal deliveries 
• Provide postpartum care for mother and baby 
• Offer family planning and reproductive health services 
• Educate mothers on nutrition, breastfeeding, and newborn care 
Qualifications 
• MSc Nursing (Obstetrics & Gynecology) 
• Midwifery certification 
Skills Required 
• Strong clinical and maternal care skills 
• Emotional support and empathy 
• Decision-making in labor situations 
• Communication and counseling 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Maternity clinics 
• Birthing centers 
• Community health centers
