56. Nurse Case Manager 
Job Summary 
A Nurse Case Manager coordinates patient care across different services, ensuring efficient, 
cost-effective, and quality treatment. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Develop and manage patient care plans 
• Coordinate between doctors, hospitals, and insurers 
• Monitor patient progress and outcomes 
• Educate patients on treatment and recovery 
• Optimize healthcare costs and resources 
• Facilitate discharge planning and follow-ups 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing 
• Case management certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Coordination and communication 
• Problem-solving 
• Patient advocacy 
• Organizational skills 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Insurance companies 
• Healthcare organizations
