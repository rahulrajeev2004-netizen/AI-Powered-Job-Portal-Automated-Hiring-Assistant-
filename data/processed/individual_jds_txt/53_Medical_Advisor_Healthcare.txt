53. Medical Advisor (Healthcare 
Companies) 
Job Summary 
A Medical Advisor (with a nursing background) provides clinical expertise to healthcare 
companies, ensuring that products, services, and strategies are medically accurate and 
effective. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide clinical insights for product development and marketing 
• Support medical content creation and validation 
• Train sales and marketing teams on medical concepts 
• Liaise with healthcare professionals and stakeholders 
• Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations 
• Participate in clinical discussions and advisory boards 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing 
• Experience in clinical practice 
• Knowledge of pharma/healthcare industry (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Clinical knowledge 
• Communication and presentation 
• Strategic thinking 
• Industry awareness 
Work Settings 
• Pharmaceutical companies 
• Medical device companies 
• Health-tech firms
