26. Operating Room (OR) Nurse 
Job Summary 
An Operating Room (OR) Nurse, also known as a scrub or circulating nurse, assists surgeons 
during surgical procedures by maintaining a sterile environment and ensuring everything runs 
smoothly in the operating theatre. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Prepare the operating room and surgical instruments 
• Ensure strict sterile techniques are followed 
• Assist surgeons during procedures (passing instruments, handling supplies) 
• Monitor patient condition during surgery 
• Count surgical instruments and materials before and after surgery 
• Maintain accurate surgical records 
• Coordinate with the surgical team (anesthetists, technicians) 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Training in operation theatre techniques (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Strong knowledge of surgical procedures 
• Attention to detail 
• Sterility and infection control expertise 
• Team coordination 
Work Settings 
• Operation theatres 
• Surgical centers 
• Multi-specialty hospitals
