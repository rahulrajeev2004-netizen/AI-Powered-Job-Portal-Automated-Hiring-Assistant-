54. Pharmaceutical Nurse Educator 
Job Summary 
A Pharmaceutical Nurse Educator trains healthcare professionals and patients on 
medications, therapies, and medical products. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Conduct training sessions on drug usage and safety 
• Educate healthcare providers about new medications 
• Provide patient education on treatment plans 
• Support clinical trials and product launches 
• Develop educational materials and presentations 
• Act as a bridge between pharma companies and healthcare providers 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing 
• Experience in clinical or pharmaceutical settings 
Skills Required 
• Teaching and presentation skills 
• Knowledge of pharmacology 
• Communication skills 
Work Settings 
• Pharmaceutical companies 
• Hospitals 
• Training organizations
