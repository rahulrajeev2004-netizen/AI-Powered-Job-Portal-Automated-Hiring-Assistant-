62. Travel Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Travel Nurse works on short-term assignments in different hospitals or regions, often 
filling staffing shortages. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide direct patient care in assigned facilities 
• Adapt quickly to new clinical environments 
• Follow hospital protocols and standards 
• Assist during staff shortages or peak demand 
• Maintain accurate patient records 
• Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Valid nursing license (local/international like NCLEX for abroad) 
• Clinical experience (1–2 years preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Adaptability and flexibility 
• Strong clinical competence 
• Quick learning ability 
• Communication skills 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Clinics 
• Emergency care units
