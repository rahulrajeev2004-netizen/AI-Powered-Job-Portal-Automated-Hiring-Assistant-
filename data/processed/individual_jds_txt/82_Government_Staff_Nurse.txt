82. Government Staff Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Government Staff Nurse provides clinical care in public healthcare facilities, ensuring 
accessible and affordable treatment to the community. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Deliver direct patient care (medication, monitoring, procedures) 
• Assist doctors in diagnosis and treatment 
• Maintain patient records and reports 
• Participate in public health programs (immunization, maternal care) 
• Follow government healthcare protocols and standards 
• Educate patients on health and hygiene 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Registered with State Nursing Council 
• Government recruitment exam (as applicable) 
Skills Required 
• Clinical competence 
• Public health awareness 
• Communication and teamwork 
Work Settings 
• Government hospitals 
• Primary Health Centers (PHCs) 
• Community Health Centers (CHCs)
