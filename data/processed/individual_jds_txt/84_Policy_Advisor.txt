84. Policy Advisor (Healthcare) 
Job Summary 
A Healthcare Policy Advisor develops and evaluates health policies and programs to 
improve healthcare systems at regional or national levels. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Research healthcare issues and trends 
• Develop policy recommendations 
• Analyze healthcare data and outcomes 
• Collaborate with government bodies and stakeholders 
• Draft reports, guidelines, and policy documents 
• Evaluate effectiveness of health programs 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing + MPH / MHA / Public Policy (preferred) 
• Experience in public health or administration 
Skills Required 
• Research and analytical skills 
• Strategic thinking 
• Knowledge of healthcare systems 
• Communication and writing 
Work Settings 
• Government ministries 
• Health departments 
• International organizations
