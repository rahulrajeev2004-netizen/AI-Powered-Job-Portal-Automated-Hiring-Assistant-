63. Cruise Ship Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Cruise Ship Nurse provides medical care to passengers and crew aboard ships, handling 
everything from minor illnesses to emergencies. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide emergency and primary care onboard 
• Respond to medical emergencies at sea 
• Administer medications and treatments 
• Maintain medical records 
• Conduct health screenings and infection control 
• Coordinate medical evacuations if needed 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing 
• Advanced certifications (BLS, ACLS, emergency care) 
• Experience in emergency/critical care 
Skills Required 
• Emergency response skills 
• Independence and decision-making 
• Crisis management 
• Cultural sensitivity 
Work Settings 
• Cruise ships 
• Maritime healthcare units
