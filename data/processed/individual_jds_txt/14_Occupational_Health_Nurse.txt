14. Occupational Health Nurse 
Job Summary 
An Occupational Health Nurse focuses on workplace health and safety, preventing work
related injuries and illnesses. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Conduct employee health assessments 
• Monitor workplace safety conditions 
• Provide first aid and emergency care 
• Implement health and safety programs 
• Educate employees on occupational hazards 
• Manage workplace injuries and rehabilitation 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Occupational health certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Knowledge of workplace safety 
• Risk assessment 
• Health education 
Work Settings 
• Factories 
• Corporate offices 
• Industrial sites
