43. Professor of Nursing 
Job Summary 
A Professor of Nursing is a senior academic responsible for teaching, research, and 
leadership in nursing education at universities or advanced institutions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Teach advanced nursing courses 
• Conduct and publish research 
• Supervise postgraduate and doctoral students 
• Lead academic departments 
• Develop advanced curriculum and policies 
• Represent institution in academic forums 
Qualifications 
• PhD in Nursing (mandatory) 
• Extensive teaching and research experience 
Skills Required 
• Advanced clinical and academic knowledge 
• Research and publication skills 
• Leadership and mentorship 
Work Settings 
• Universities 
• Research institutions 
• Nursing colleges
