80. Behavioral Health Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Behavioral Health Nurse focuses on behavioral patterns, emotional disorders, and 
mental health conditions, helping patients manage behaviors affecting their well-being. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assess behavioral and psychological conditions 
• Develop and implement behavioral care plans 
• Monitor patient behavior and progress 
• Assist in therapy sessions (CBT, behavioral therapy) 
• Administer medications as prescribed 
• Support patients in developing healthy coping mechanisms 
• Work with families for holistic care 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Specialization in mental/behavioral health (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Observation and analytical skills 
• Emotional intelligence 
• Communication and counseling 
• Behavioral therapy knowledge 
Work Settings 
• Mental health hospitals 
• Rehabilitation centers 
• Community health centers 
• Schools
