45. Nurse Researcher 
Job Summary 
A Nurse Researcher conducts scientific studies to improve patient care, healthcare systems, 
and nursing practices using evidence-based methodologies. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and conduct research studies 
• Collect, analyze, and interpret healthcare data 
• Publish research papers in journals 
• Develop evidence-based nursing practices 
• Apply for research grants and funding 
• Collaborate with multidisciplinary research teams 
Qualifications 
• MSc Nursing (minimum) 
• PhD in Nursing (preferred for advanced roles) 
Skills Required 
• Research methodology 
• Statistical analysis 
• Critical thinking 
• Academic writing 
Work Settings 
• Universities 
• Research institutions 
• Healthcare organizations
