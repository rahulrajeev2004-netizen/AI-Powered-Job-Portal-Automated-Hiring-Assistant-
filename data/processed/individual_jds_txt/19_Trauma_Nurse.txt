19. Trauma Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Trauma Nurse specializes in treating patients with severe, life-threatening injuries caused 
by accidents, violence, or disasters. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform rapid trauma assessment (ABCDE approach) 
• Control bleeding and manage shock 
• Assist in emergency surgeries 
• Stabilize patients before ICU transfer 
• Coordinate with trauma teams (surgeons, ER staff) 
• Provide post-trauma care and monitoring 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Trauma care certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• High-pressure response capability 
• Strong emergency and surgical knowledge 
• Quick thinking and prioritization 
Work Settings 
• Trauma centers 
• Emergency departments 
• Disaster response units
