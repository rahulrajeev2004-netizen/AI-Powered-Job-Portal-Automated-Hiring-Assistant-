36. Nursing Supervisor 
Job Summary 
A Nursing Supervisor oversees multiple nursing units or shifts, ensuring smooth hospital 
operations and proper coordination among departments. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Supervise nursing staff across different departments 
• Ensure compliance with hospital policies 
• Coordinate between doctors, nurses, and administration 
• Handle emergencies and critical situations 
• Monitor patient care quality across units 
• Provide guidance and support to staff nurses 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing 
• Several years of clinical experience 
Skills Required 
• Strong leadership 
• Decision-making under pressure 
• Coordination and multitasking 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Multi-specialty healthcare centers
