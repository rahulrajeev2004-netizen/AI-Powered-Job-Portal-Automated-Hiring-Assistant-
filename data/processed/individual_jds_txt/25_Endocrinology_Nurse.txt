25. Endocrinology Nurse 
Job Summary 
An Endocrinology Nurse focuses on patients with hormonal disorders such as diabetes, 
thyroid diseases, and metabolic conditions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor blood glucose levels and hormone-related parameters 
• Educate patients on insulin therapy and medication use 
• Assist in managing chronic endocrine conditions 
• Support lifestyle and dietary counseling 
• Monitor complications of endocrine disorders 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Endocrinology or diabetes educator certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Knowledge of endocrine system 
• Patient counseling and education 
• Long-term care management 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Diabetes clinics 
• Endocrinology centers
