32. Labor & Delivery Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Labor & Delivery Nurse assists women during childbirth, ensuring safe delivery and 
providing emotional and clinical support throughout labor. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor contractions and fetal heart rate 
• Assist doctors during labor and childbirth 
• Administer medications (pain relief, IV fluids) 
• Support mothers physically and emotionally 
• Handle normal deliveries (in some settings) 
• Provide immediate newborn care after birth 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Labor and delivery training (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Quick decision-making 
• Emotional support and empathy 
• Crisis management (complications during labor) 
Work Settings 
• Delivery rooms 
• Maternity wards 
• Birthing centers
