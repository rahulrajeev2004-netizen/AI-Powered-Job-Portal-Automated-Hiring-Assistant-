59. Legal Nurse Consultant 
Job Summary 
A Legal Nurse Consultant assists lawyers and legal teams by interpreting medical records and 
providing expert insights in medical-related legal cases. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Review and analyze medical records 
• Identify standards of care and possible negligence 
• Prepare medical reports for legal cases 
• Assist attorneys in understanding medical terminology 
• Provide expert opinions in court cases 
• Support litigation and case preparation 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing 
• Legal nurse consulting certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Analytical thinking 
• Strong documentation skills 
• Knowledge of legal systems 
• Communication skills 
Work Settings 
• Law firms 
• Insurance companies 
• Independent consulting
