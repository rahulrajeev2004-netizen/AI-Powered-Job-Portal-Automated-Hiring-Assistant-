77. Health Care Assistant (HCA) 
Job Summary 
A Health Care Assistant supports nurses and healthcare professionals in delivering day-to
day patient care, especially in long-term and community settings. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assist with personal care and hygiene 
• Help patients with mobility and daily activities 
• Monitor patient conditions and report changes 
• Provide emotional and social support 
• Maintain cleanliness and patient comfort 
• Assist in basic medical procedures 
Qualifications 
• Certificate in healthcare assistance 
• On-the-job training 
Skills Required 
• Empathy and patience 
• Communication skills 
• Teamwork 
• Basic caregiving skills 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Nursing homes 
• Home care 
• Community health centers
