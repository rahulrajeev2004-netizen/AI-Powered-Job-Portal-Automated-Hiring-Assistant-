55. Insurance Nurse 
Job Summary 
An Insurance Nurse works with insurance companies to evaluate medical claims, assess 
patient cases, and ensure appropriate healthcare coverage decisions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Review medical records and insurance claims 
• Assess treatment necessity and coverage eligibility 
• Coordinate with hospitals and doctors 
• Detect fraudulent claims 
• Provide recommendations on claim approvals or denials 
• Ensure compliance with insurance policies 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Experience in clinical care 
Skills Required 
• Analytical thinking 
• Knowledge of medical terminology 
• Attention to detail 
• Decision-making 
Work Settings 
• Insurance companies 
• Third-party administrators (TPA) 
• Healthcare firms
