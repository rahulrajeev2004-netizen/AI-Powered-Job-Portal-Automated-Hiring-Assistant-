27. Perioperative Nurse 
Job Summary 
A Perioperative Nurse provides comprehensive care to surgical patients before, during, and 
after surgery, ensuring patient safety and smooth surgical outcomes. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Conduct pre-operative assessments and prepare patients 
• Educate patients about surgical procedures and recovery 
• Assist during surgery (as scrub or circulating nurse) 
• Monitor patients in post-anesthesia recovery 
• Manage pain and prevent complications 
• Ensure continuity of care across all surgical phases 
Qualifications 
• BSc Nursing / GNM 
• Perioperative nursing certification (preferred) 
Skills Required 
• Holistic patient care 
• Surgical knowledge 
• Communication and patient education 
• Critical thinking 
Work Settings 
• Hospitals 
• Surgical units 
• Day-care surgery centers
